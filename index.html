About Me

Welcome to my website.
I am currently engaged in multiple projects in the field of applied Mathematics. That usually means either developing new numerical methods for differential equations or applications of machine learning into real life problems. My main interest is to bring value for industry or the end-users. I am mostly interested in the numerical analysis and the complexity of continuous problems. My research is mainly conducted within the Information-Based Complexity framework.

I am also interested in other practical applications of mathematics, such as data science, machine learning, deep learning, data mining, time series, parallel computation (mostly CUDA), predictive analytics, and optimization.
