About Me


I am currently research-and-teaching assistant at the Faculty of Applied Mathematics, AGH University of Science and Technology.

I am mostly interested in the numerical analysis and the complexity (in the sense of Information-Based Complexity) of continuous problems connected to stochastic analysis. My research is mainly conducted within the approximation of stochastic problems.

Currently, I am Team Leader of Data Scientists Departament in company connected with application predictive maintance in industry. I have a theoretical and practical knowledge in the fields of artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, time series, deep learning, stochastic analysis and numerical methods.

In addition to ML experience, I participate in the international AGH-NVIDIA project on construction and analysis of algorithms for approximate solving of SDEs together with its efficient implementation on GPU NVIDIA graphics cards (parallel computation - mostly CUDA). One of the outcomes of the project is going to be a library (written in CUDA C) dedicated for GPU-powered computations.
